# Governance

## Overview

The **System-of-Systems Digital Twin** is developed within the CRETE VALLEY
research initiative at the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA),
Electric Power Systems Laboratory (EPU). It is being submitted for hosting under
[LF Energy](https://lfenergy.org/) at the **Sandbox** stage.

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| **Maintainer** | Has merge rights. Reviews and approves pull requests. Responsible for releases. |
| **Contributor** | Anyone who submits a pull request, files an issue, or improves documentation. |
| **User** | Anyone who runs or integrates the software. |

## Decision Making

This project follows a **do-ocracy**: decisions are made by the people doing the
work. For significant changes (API redesign, new dependencies, breaking changes,
adding/removing a component) a 72-hour comment period on the relevant issue or PR
is required before merging.

Technical governance is intentionally **separate from organisational/business
governance**, in line with LF Energy requirements. Anyone may participate in the
technical community regardless of financial membership.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Any contributor with a sustained record of quality contributions may be proposed
as a maintainer by an existing maintainer. Approval requires a majority vote
among current maintainers with a one-week voting period.

## Meetings

Project meetings are open to all. Meeting notes and decisions are recorded in the
repository under `docs/meetings/`.

## Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
(version 2.1). Current maintainers responsible for enforcement are listed in
[MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

## Amendments

This document may be amended by a pull request approved by a majority of current
maintainers.
